OUR STORY

Born from Passion

AMAO was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to create swimwear that combines bold design with exceptional comfort. Our name, meaning "love" in Portuguese, reflects our passion for the ocean and beach culture.

Handcrafted Quality

Each piece is carefully designed in Portugal and crafted from premium materials that stand up to sun, salt, and sand while maintaining their shape and color season after season.

Sustainable Practices

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact by using eco-friendly fabrics and responsible manufacturing processes without compromising on style or quality.

OUR MISSION

To empower women to feel confident and beautiful in swimwear that celebrates their individuality. We believe every body is a beach body, and our designs are made to flatter all shapes and sizes.
